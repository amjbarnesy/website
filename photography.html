Architecture

Buildings are portraits of the people who made them. I look for geometry, shadow and the quiet drama of a structure doing exactly what it was designed to do, and I try to get there before anyone else clutters the frame.

Food Photography

Chocolate worked by hand, ingredients placed with intention, textures that stop the scroll. Food photography is about making people feel something before they taste anything. I work closely with makers and producers to find the shot that does the craft genuine justice.

Business events, networking rooms and conference stages. The moments that matter in a professional context are easy to miss and impossible to recreate. I work quickly and unobtrusively to capture the energy, the speakers and the connections that make an event worth attending.
