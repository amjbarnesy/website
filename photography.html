Inspired Photography by Adam

Architecture. Events and festivals. Theatre and people.

Prestige buildings from London to Cambridge. Official photographer to Firstlight. Festival work at Latitude. The kind of work that only comes from knowing when to wait, when to move, and when to just press the shutter. The full body of work lives here.

Commercial Photography

Business events, networking rooms and conference stages. The moments that matter in a professional context are easy to miss and impossible to recreate. I work quickly and unobtrusively to capture the energy, the speakers and the connections that make an event worth attending.
